About Hindalco Hospital Renukoot

Hindalco Hospital in Renukoot, Uttar Pradesh, is a multi-specialty hospital that stands as a significant part of Hindalco Industries' corporate social responsibility (CSR) initiatives. Equipped with modern medical technology and staffed by experienced professionals, the hospital delivers exceptional healthcare to both the employees of Hindalco Industries and the wider community in Sonbhadra.

With a capacity of around 150 beds, Hindalco Hospital specializes in treatments including dermatology, cosmetic procedures, and laser treatments. Key services offered include acne treatment, anti-aging procedures, and care for eczema and scars. The hospital is also recognized for its dedicated COVID-19 treatment facilities, complete with ICU beds.

Our infrastructure includes advanced surgical and diagnostic equipment, supporting a wide range of medical treatments. We provide specialized consultations, laser treatments, and a comprehensive suite of healthcare services.

About Hindalco Colony Renukoot

Hindalco Colony in Renukoot is a well-developed residential area built to support the employees of Hindalco Industries. The colony is equipped with essential amenities such as schools, recreational areas, parks, shopping complexes, and healthcare facilities, ensuring a comfortable and fulfilling lifestyle for residents. The community focuses on creating a safe and thriving environment for families and individuals alike.

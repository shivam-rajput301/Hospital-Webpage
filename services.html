Our Hindalco Services

At Hindalco Hospital Renukoot, we offer a wide range of medical services to meet the healthcare needs of our patients.

Cardiology

We offer advanced cardiac care including diagnostics, treatment, and rehabilitation.

Neurology

Expert neurological care including diagnosis and treatment of disorders affecting the brain and nervous system.

Orthopedics

Our orthopedic team specializes in treating musculoskeletal issues including fractures and joint problems.

Pediatrics

Comprehensive medical care for infants, children, and adolescents with a focus on growth and development.

ENT (Ear, Nose, and Throat)

Specialized care for ear, nose, and throat conditions, including diagnosis and treatment.

Emergency Care

24/7 emergency services to handle all urgent medical conditions with expert care and rapid response.
